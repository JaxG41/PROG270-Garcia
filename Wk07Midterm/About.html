About Seahawk Fans

This is the about our fans page.

Now more than ever we are LOUDER, PROUDER, FASTER, STRONGER

It has an animated graphic on it.



Seattle Seahawk Fans are a rare breed indeed. This year has been an amazing one for the Seahawks and their fans. On September 15, 2013 the 12th Man made Guiness Book of World Records history by setting the record for loudest stadium, not once but twice!

The record came in the first half with a decibel level of 131.9. Then in the third quarter, the 12th Man saw the 131.9 - and raised it to 136.6. When fans like you make noise like that for our Seattle Seahawks, they will be difficult if not impossible to beat. Thank you Seattle Seahawk Fans!
