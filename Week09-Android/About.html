About ASUS Memo Pad

This is the About page.

With Google Play Store you can download any app. You can get easy access to some of your favorite applications by creating app shortcuts on your Home Screen. Free up space for new app shortcuts by removing existing app shortcuts on your Home Screen. View the detailed app information while creating a shortcut for the app. While holding an app on the All Apps screen, the display switches to Home Screen and the App info appears on the top of the Home Screen display. Drag the app to App info to display its detailed information. You can also create folders to organzie your shortcuts on your Home Screen. The Recents screen allows you to view a list of app shortcuts base from your recently launched apps. Using the Recents Screen you can easily go back to or switch betweeen the apps you recently launced. Download the E-Manuel to learn more: ASUS MeMo Pad E-Manual
